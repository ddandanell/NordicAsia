Update the homepage with area coverage, network value, and stronger front-page storytelling

Update the homepage of the Nordic community and business network website. The homepage must feel modern, premium, clear, and mobile first. The goal is that a new visitor instantly understands what this community is, where it is active, who it is for, and why it is valuable.

The design should feel clean, Scandinavian, trustworthy, and social, but still business-relevant. Use strong visual hierarchy, large readable headings, bold cards, clear call-to-action buttons, beautiful photography, and a simple structure that is easy to understand even for someone young or completely new to the concept.

Main homepage goals:
	1.	Show clearly what the community is
	2.	Show where the network is active in Indonesia
	3.	Show why people and companies join
	4.	Show the value of the network, contacts, events, and member access
	5.	Make the platform feel alive, useful, and high quality

Please update the homepage with these sections:
	1.	Hero section
Create a strong hero section with:

	•	A short and powerful headline
	•	A very simple explanation of what this network is
	•	A supporting sentence that explains this is a Nordic community and business network in Indonesia for people from Denmark, Sweden, Norway, Finland, and Iceland
	•	Two clear buttons:
	•	Join the Community
	•	Explore How It Works

The hero must make it obvious that this is the place for people who want both business and real human connection.
	2.	Area coverage section
Add a strong homepage section showing the areas where the community is active in Indonesia.

This section must:
	•	Show area cards such as Bali, Jakarta, and other key Indonesian cities or areas where Europeans and Nordic professionals are active
	•	Include a nice image for each area
	•	Show the name of the area with a bold visual effect
	•	Show the number of signed-up members or registered users in each area
	•	Include a short descriptive text for each area explaining why the area matters and what kind of people are there
	•	Make the section visually attractive and easy to scan on mobile

Examples of area card content:
	•	Bali: Creative founders, remote workers, families, hospitality people, and long-stay professionals
	•	Jakarta: Corporate professionals, executives, decision-makers, embassy-related people, and business network activity

Also add a short intro text above this section explaining:
“These are the areas where our Nordic network is active in Indonesia. Explore where people are based, where events happen, and where the community is growing.”
	3.	What you find inside the community
Add a section called:
“What You Find Inside”

This section should clearly explain that inside the community people get:
	•	Knowledge and practical help
	•	Useful contacts
	•	A large network of like-minded people from Denmark, Sweden, Norway, Finland, and Iceland
	•	Access to events across Indonesia
	•	Access to companies, professionals, founders, and people who have already built things and know the market
	•	A place to ask questions, get recommendations, and connect faster

This must be written very clearly and in plain English.
	4.	Why this community exists
Add a section explaining the bigger idea:
This is the place for people who want to do business, build relationships, and also meet real people. The goal is to create a stronger Nordic community in Indonesia where people help each other, share knowledge, build business opportunities, and create a better network together.

This section should explain:
	•	We are stronger as a group
	•	A good network helps both companies and individuals
	•	The community makes it easier to settle in, connect, and build trust
	•	The larger and stronger the network becomes, the more useful it becomes for everyone

	5.	Big company trust section
Add a section that shows the quality of the network by referencing major companies already connected to the ecosystem.

This section should:
	•	Mention that many of the biggest companies from each Nordic country are part of or connected to this ecosystem
	•	Show six example company logos or placeholder company names from major Nordic business players
	•	Make it clear this is only to show the level and seriousness of the network
	•	Include supporting text like:
“Trusted by professionals, founders, and people connected to some of the most recognized Nordic companies active in Indonesia.”

Do not make false claims. Use placeholder logos or editable logo slots if needed.
	6.	Final call to action
End with a strong section that invites people to join the network.

The message should be:
If you want business opportunities, better contacts, useful events, and a stronger Nordic community around you in Indonesia, this is where you start.

Design requirements:
	•	Mobile first
	•	Premium but simple
	•	Strong spacing and visual hierarchy
	•	Easy to understand
	•	Reusable card components
	•	Section-based structure
	•	Realistic placeholder content
	•	Clean Scandinavian styling
	•	Photos should feel real, warm, professional, and social
	•	Make it look production-ready

Do not build backend logic yet.
Only update the frontend and content structure.
